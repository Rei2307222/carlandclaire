Founder

Nouva Puspita

Carl & Claire was founded by Nouva Puspita, inspired by her love for fragrances and a desire to fill the gap in the market for affordable yet high-quality perfumes. Her passion stemmed from personal experiences exploring scents during her journey of self-discovery. Nouva started small, selling perfumes to friends, at bazaars, and eventually in a mall, before fully embracing online platforms.

The brand faced early challenges, like launching too many variants at once, but Nouva quickly learned the importance of customer feedback. By refining her offerings and prioritizing elegant, versatile scents that suit Indonesia's warm climate, Carl & Claire grew into a trusted perfume brand known for its accessibility and thoughtful craftsmanship.
